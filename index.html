About MediTech Hire

Bridging the gap between exceptional talent and innovative companies

Your Trusted Recruitment Partner Since 2013

MediTech Hire was established in 2013 and has evolved into a leading business recruitment partner with numerous reputed medical and technical companies. We have successfully placed thousands of employees across diverse domains including medical, technical, and business sectors.

Our expertise spans across Claims Processing, Medical Coding, Medical Scribing, Software Development, Clinical Data Management, BPO/KPO services, and many other specialized fields. We work with organizations ranging from MSMEs to MNCs, providing 24/7 support to our clients.

Vision & Mission

"To provide the right candidate at the right time at the right place, strengthening organizations from MSMEs to MNCs in terms of business stability and income generation. We ensure that once hired, employees contribute to long-term success."
